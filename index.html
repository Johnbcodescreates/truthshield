The Problem

Scams Are Becoming
Harder to Recognize

Modern fraud has evolved. Today's scams are crafted with AI, personalized, and designed to exploit trust. The old rules don't apply anymore.

🤖

AI-Generated Scams

Fraudsters now use generative AI to craft convincing emails, messages, and voice calls that are nearly indistinguishable from legitimate communication.

💼

Fake Job Listings

Fraudulent recruiters post realistic job offers to harvest personal information, collect fees, or launder money through unsuspecting applicants.

🎣

Phishing Attacks

Sophisticated phishing emails and texts mimic banks, government agencies, and trusted brands to steal login credentials and financial information.

🔐

Identity & Financial Theft

Once criminals have your personal data, the damage compounds — drained accounts, ruined credit, and years of recovery efforts.

Protection Areas

Protection for Everyday
Situations

Scams appear in many forms. TruthShield covers the situations you actually encounter.

💼

Job or Work Opportunity

Fake job listings are increasingly used to collect personal and financial information from unsuspecting applicants.

✉️

Email or Text Message

Phishing campaigns use urgent language and spoofed identities to steal credentials and financial data via inbox.
